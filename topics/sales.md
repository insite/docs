# Sales

The Billing toolkit (customer-facing name: **Sales**) generates invoices and collects online payments. Use it to invoice for class or event registrations, product sales, and application fees, and to take payment through a processing service such as Bambora.

> More Billing pages are coming. For now, this overview is the only customer-facing page in the section.
